My love, it's so easy to keep my head down
It's so easy to make believe the beautiful life I had
It's faded away

But, the small moments, the silent spaces
They pull me back and bring tears to my eyes
Because in those spaces, where the waking world fades away
In those sleepy moments I remember an unabashed yawn
I remember a glance over a shoulder and a smile hidden in the corners of full red lips

The past weighs heavy
So I must keep the present
Much heavier still.
